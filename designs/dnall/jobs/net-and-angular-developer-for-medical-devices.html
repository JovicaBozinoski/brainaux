Senior .NET with Angular Developer for Medical Devices

About the project

Our partner is the world leader in integration solutions for biomedical devices for hospitals and healthcare organizations. Through its connectivity solutions, advanced integration, and analysis options, they unlock the full potential of biomedical data by making it intelligible and usable for clinical and operational decision-making. With more than 25 years in the industry, the company has established strong partnerships with major manufacturers of medical devices and is present in more than 2000 hospitals in 39 countries.

Job description

Responsibilities

  • Reporting to the development manager, you are involved in the development of software within the Web team.
  • You work on new product features, as well as on the maintenance of existing versions.
  • You work in a modern environment with cutting-edge technologies.
  • Construction of a technical solution from the functional specifications
  • Participation in technological choices in accordance with the architectural choices in force established by the project managers
  • Software development respecting development standards and procedures
  • Integration and development
  • Writing of technical documentation

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in C # and Web development, especially on Visual Studio platforms
  • Proficiency in raw CSS and the ability to create custom layouts and components without using a CSS library
  • Extensive experience in Angular and TypeScript
  • Experience in setting up and deploying Azure cloud solutions would be a plus
  • Ability to communicate in English, both written and spoken
  • Organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Ability to quickly integrate new methodologies.
