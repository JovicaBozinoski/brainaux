Quality Assurance Analyst for a Delivery Company

About the project

Our partner is a one of the largest independent distributor in USA and Canada. They serves more than 12,000 retail stores throughout the United States and Canada, and over 50 countries around the world.

Job Description

The Quality Assurance Analyst is responsible for Quality Assurance reviews at each stage of the Software Development Life Cycle exercising the fundamentals of software testing. Primary expert focus is exercised in the preventive quality control process that meets the software requirements. This includes Test Planning, design, employ Tools, Techniques and Test Metrics to ensure successful QA review with maximum test coverage within time and budget.

Core Responsibilities

  • Conducts quality assurance review of applications throughout the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC).
  • Reviews Requirements to ensure that they are clear, specific and testable
  • Assist in creating Test Plan, Testing Technique and Testing Procedures
  • Analyse and Design Test Cases
  • Create Dataset to test logic, error handling and System workflow
  • Identify, reproduce, isolate and manage defects/bugs and verify their resolution.
  • Conducts (functional/non-functional) tests on systems developed and implemented, and determines suitability for production.
  • Write SQL Scripts to Analyse Database Testing
  • Assist in creating and manage Software Testware
  • Conduct Web Service/API (integration) Testing
  • Ensure user requirements are met before the implementation of new system releases/patches.
  • Assist in preparing and Managing UAT
  • Provide support during User Acceptance Testing (UAT).
  • Work closely with Business Analysts, Project Managers/Scrum Masters, Developers, Product Owners and other QA Analysts in a team environment
  • Diagnose the Severity of issues found
  • Prepare test summary reports for all assigned projects/systems (indicating test status/coverage and metrics)
  • Assists in training for clients in System Acceptance Testing (SAT).
  • Assist in training and provide support during User Acceptance Testing (UAT).
  • Reviews user and operations manuals to ensure clarity and completeness.

Competencies

  • Demonstrates Strong Knowledge of Software Development Life Cycle Methodologies
  • Demonstrates Strong Knowledge of the Fundamentals of Software Testing
  • Strong Testing Technique
  • Hands-on in ICT tools
  • Demonstrates Strong Analytical and Problem Solving skills
  • Good in Test Estimation
  • Sound understanding of Database Testing including the ability to write SQL statements
  • Working Knowledge of applicable Industry standards and best practices
  • Demonstrate Strong Oral & Written Communication
  • Knowledge of Integration testing and ability verify components
  • Excellent Planning and Organizing skills
  • Effective Team Player
  • Good Coding ability
