.NET/.NET Core Developer for Medical Devices

About the project

Our partner is the world leader in integration solutions for biomedical devices for hospitals and healthcare organizations. Through its connectivity solutions, advanced integration, and analysis options, they unlock the full potential of biomedical data by making it intelligible and usable for clinical and operational decision-making. With more than 25 years in the industry, the company has established strong partnerships with major manufacturers of medical devices and is present in more than 2000 hospitals in 39 countries.

Job description

As an experienced software engineer, you will mainly work on the security aspects of the product that our partner develops.

Responsibilities

  • Software development respecting development standards and procedures
  • Fix the product security vulnerabilities according to the SW security specifications
  • Initiate (and participate in) code reviews within the SW development team
  • Write technical documentation, SW designs and Test plans
  • Write unit tests (XUnit)
  • Work closely with QA Team to better identify the impact of your developments
  • Estimate required time for tasks and monitor the time spent on them regularly
  • Development on the .Net Framework/Core
  • Monitoring of vulnerabilities on WhiteSource (Mend)
  • Code analysis tools (Sonar)
