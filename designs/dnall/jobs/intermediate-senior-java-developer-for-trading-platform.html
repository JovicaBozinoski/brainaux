Intermediate / Senior Java Developer for trading platform

About the project

Our partner is a global online brokerage firm, specializing in providing multi-asset trading solutions to thousands of clients around the world. Its mission is to disrupt the brokerage industry by allowing access to global markets and thousands of products to allow our clients to trade the world in the palm of their hands.

Responsibilities

  • Contribute in all phases of the development lifecycle
  • Analyze functional and non-functional requirements
  • Research and advise in the selection and integration of the best technologies for our portfolio
  • Design, build, and maintain efficient, scalable, reusable, and reliable Java code
  • Prepare and produce releases of software components
  • Identify bottlenecks and devise permanent solutions
  • Actively troubleshoot and resolve issues
  • Produce and maintain technical documentation of the developed modules/applications
  • Work closely with other teams and project stakeholders

Requirements

  • Degree in Computer Science or a related field
  • Minimum of 3 years of software development experience with Java
  • Experience with Hibernate and Spring framework
  • Good understanding of design patterns and software architecture principles
  • Experience with relational and non-relational databases
  • Experience with websockets RESTful APIs
  • Knowledge of Linux commands and scripts
  • Understanding of the FIX protocol is an advantage
