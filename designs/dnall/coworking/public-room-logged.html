What is co-working space & how it works?

Coworking is an arrangement in which workers for different companies share an office space, allowing cost savings and convenience through the use of common infrastructures, such as equipment, utilities and receptionist and custodial services, and in some cases refreshments and parcel acceptance services.

Can I bring a pet with me?

Consult with your workplace about associated policies and requirements to see if bringing your dog to work is appropriate and allowed. Some work environments may not be appropriate or safe for dogs. Check if anyone at your workplace is allergic to dogs, and discuss with them if bringing your dog to work would adversely impact their health.

Ensure the office environment is safe for pets. Cables, cords and rubbish bins can be hazardous for pets, so ensure dogs in the office can't access these. Supervise your dog and make sure they won't be able to escape and get lost by accident.

Is there any kind of deposit required or any minimum commitment?

No, it's a tradition, strangely, with no legal basis. It demonstrates the buyer's commitment to the purchase and is incorporated into the contract for sale and purchase, for the benefit of the seller.

A deposit is usually 10% of the purchase price, a significant sum. The deposit is paid to the seller on exchange of contracts as part payment of the purchase price.
